FAQ

Questions we get asked before the call.

Is there a minimum contract period?
No. Every package is month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel with 30 days' notice — no termination fees, no lock-in clauses. We'd rather earn your subscription every month than trap you in one.
Is the monthly price fixed once agreed?
Yes. After your free consultation we confirm your exact scope and price in writing. That rate is fixed for the duration of your subscription — no mystery billing, no scope-creep invoices. If your business changes significantly (new sites, new headcount), we'll review together and agree any changes openly.
What if I only need one-off help, not a subscription?
We offer one-off add-on services for businesses that just need a specific deliverable — a single RA, a bizSAFE application, a safety audit, or an incident investigation. If you later decide you'd like ongoing support, we can credit a portion of the one-off fee toward your first subscription month.
How does the appointed WSHO service work?
Under the Overwatch tier, we're formally named as your appointed Workplace Safety and Health Officer on MOM records — the same role a full-time WSHO fills in larger companies. This gives you the compliance standing required for certain project tenders and higher-risk operations, at a fraction of a full-time salary. You get bi-weekly on-site presence, direct MOM correspondence handling, and 24h emergency response.
What happens in the free consultation?
A 30-minute call or meet — your choice — where we ask about your business: what you do, how many staff, your current safety setup, and what's worrying you. By the end you'll know exactly which package fits, roughly what it'll cost, and what the first month looks like. No pressure to sign up on the call.
What sectors do you work with?
Our sweet spot is Singapore SMEs in F&B, workshops, retail, warehouses, offices, and light industrial. We also support construction-adjacent businesses through the Overwatch tier where an appointed WSHO is required. Not sure if we're a fit? Book a call — we'll give you a straight answer.
Are prices GST-inclusive?
No. All prices shown are GST-exclusive (before 9% GST). We'll confirm the GST-inclusive total in your formal quotation after the consultation.
