FAQ

Questions we get asked before the call.

Is there a minimum contract period?
No. Every package is month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel with 30 days' notice — no termination fees, no lock-in clauses. We'd rather earn your subscription every month than trap you in one. Note: the one-time onboarding fee is non-refundable, but is waived entirely on a 12-month commitment.
What is the onboarding fee for?
The first month involves roughly 8 hours of work before regular service begins — initial site walkthrough, document collection, compliance folder setup, and full risk profile scoping. This is charged once as S$800 (Watch), S$1,200 (Guard), or S$1,500 (Overwatch). Commit to 12 months upfront and we waive it completely.
Is the monthly price fixed once agreed?
Yes. After your free consultation we confirm your exact scope and price in writing. That rate is fixed for the duration of your subscription — no mystery billing, no scope-creep invoices. If your business changes significantly (new sites, new headcount), we'll review together and agree any changes openly.
What is the out-of-scope rate and when does it apply?
Any work outside your agreed monthly scope is charged at S$150/hour. We always tell you and agree in writing before starting any out-of-scope work — you will never receive an unexpected invoice. Common examples: additional incident investigations beyond the monthly allowance, extra site visits, new ISO certifications, or noise and audiometry testing.
What if I only need one-off help, not a subscription?
We offer one-off add-on services for businesses that just need a specific deliverable — a single RA from S$650, a bizSAFE application from S$2,400, a safety audit from S$1,800, or an incident investigation from S$1,200. If you later decide you'd like ongoing support, we can credit a portion of the one-off fee toward your first subscription month.
How does the appointed WSHO service work?
Under the Overwatch tier, we're formally named as your appointed Workplace Safety and Health Officer on MOM records — the same role a full-time WSHO fills in larger companies. This gives you the compliance standing required for certain project tenders and higher-risk operations, at a fraction of a full-time salary (S$2,500/month vs S$90,000+/year for a senior WSHO including CPF). You get weekly on-site presence, direct MOM correspondence handling, unlimited incident investigations, and 24h emergency response.
What happens in the free consultation?
A 30-minute call or meet — your choice — where we ask about your business: what you do, how many staff, your current safety setup, and what's worrying you. By the end you'll know exactly which package fits, roughly what it'll cost, and what the first month looks like. No pressure to sign up on the call.
What sectors do you work with?
Our sweet spot is Singapore SMEs in F&B, workshops, retail, warehouses, offices, and light industrial. We also support construction-adjacent and manufacturing businesses through the Overwatch tier where an appointed WSHO is required. For high-risk sectors (marine, petrochemical, construction), the Overwatch tier starts from S$3,200/month. Not sure if we're a fit? Book a call — we'll give you a straight answer.
Are prices GST-inclusive?
No. All prices shown are GST-exclusive (before 9% GST). We'll confirm the GST-inclusive total in your formal quotation after the consultation.
